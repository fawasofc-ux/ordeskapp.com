About Ordesk

Eliminate the Friction of Switching Between Tasks

Ordesk is designed for professionals who juggle multiple projects throughout the day. Instead of manually reopening apps and repositioning windows every time you switch contexts, Ordesk saves your exact setup and restores it instantly.

  • Capture your current desktop — open apps and window positions across all displays
  • Save setups as reusable workspaces you can launch anytime
  • Restore everything with a single click or keyboard shortcut
  • Minimize or close non-essential apps automatically for a clean desk
